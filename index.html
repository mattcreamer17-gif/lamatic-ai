The Eduba frame

We split every AI problem into three layers. Roughly 60% of what ships as “agent work” is traditional code and database work. Roughly 30% is rule-based logic. About 10% is genuine LLM work.

60% code & DB 30% rules 10% LLM

Teams that skip the split waste budget putting language models where Postgres would do the job faster, cheaper, and with better guarantees. Applied to Lamatic: your platform already makes the 10% easy. The sell upmarket depends on helping your customers (and your own team) draw the line between the three layers before they open the canvas. That is an authoring pattern, not a feature.
